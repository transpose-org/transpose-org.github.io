Contributor Ladder

We welcome contributors at all experience levels. Progress through our contributor ladder as you deepen your involvement with TRANSPOSE:

1. Community Member

Join discussions, report issues, and provide feedback on TDToolkit components. No coding required.

2. Contributor (Triage)

Help triage issues, improve documentation, and assist new users. Gain familiarity with our codebase and processes.

3. Contributor (Pull Requests)

Submit bug fixes and feature implementations. Work closely with maintainers on code review and testing.

4. Component Maintainer

Lead development of specific TDToolkit components. Review PRs, manage releases, and guide technical direction.
