TDToolkit provides researchers and practitioners with standardized tools for travel demand modeling, enabling reproducible benchmarking across classical discrete choice models and modern machine learning approaches. Our modular ecosystem ensures consistent evaluation metrics, shared data schemas, and transparent methodologies.
